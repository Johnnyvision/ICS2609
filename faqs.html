FAQS

How old do I have to be to rent a car?
- You must be at least 18 years old.

What documents do I need to rent a car?
- Valid driver's license, Valid ID, and proof of insurance.

How much does it cost to rent a car?
- The cost will depend on the type of vehicle and the duration of the rental period.

Can I return the car early?
- Yes, but you may be charged a different rate compared to the initial agreement.

Can I pick up the car at one location and return it to another?
- Yes, but additional fees will be charged.

Do I need to refill the gas tank before returning the car?
- Yes, you are required to fill the car with a full tank. Failure to comply will result in additional charges.

What happens if I get into an accident while driving the rental car?
- If you get into an accident, contact the rental service immediately and follow our instructions. A filed report from the police and your insurance company is required.

Car Rental Rules

- Renters need to be at least 18 years old.

- Renters should have a valid Driver's license in possession.

- Renters should have insurance either through the rental company or personal

- Renters are responsible for refueling the car before returning it to the company.

- Renters will be charged for late returns and committed violations.
